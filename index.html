Do you love delicious, out of this world burgers? Of course you do. It's easy to see why all extraterrestrial life  loves them too.

In the near future, Earth promotes intergalactic peace by running an exceptional take out  food delivery service. Using delivery drones capable of moving at 5 times the speed of light,  we are able to guarantee fresh, never frozen burgers, with premium toppings, delivered to any planet in 15 seconds or less.

The best drone operators avoid crashing their drones, while consistently beating Earth's 15 second guarantee.
