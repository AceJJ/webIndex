Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09/28/18

Please read this privacy policy carefully. This privacy policy is in place for all users of our website and mobile apps. Use of our website or one of our mobile apps constitutes agreement with the policy. If at anytime, you find that you disagree with any part of this policy, please immediately stop using our website and mobile apps.

Our storage of Personal Data
We do not store the personal data of any user on our servers. Game save/high score data is stored locally on users devices.

Third Party Usage of Data
While visiting our site, or using our mobile games, third party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve personalized ad content to our users. Information may be tracked about a user visit to our website or app, and information may be used to determine ads based on a user's prior visits other websites that is known to third parties. These advertising cookies help third parties to provide the most relevant ads to users, based on their visits to our site or app or other sites or apps.

Opting out of Personalized Advertising
All users may opt of personalized advertisement from third parties while our site and mobile app. Note that if you do choose to opt, you will still see ads. The ads you receive will however, be less relevant to you, since they will not be based on your history on the internet. To opt out, please adjust yourAd Settings. To limit ad tracking in apps for iPhone, adjust your Advertising setting. In iPhone Settings, select Privacy > Advertising. Then turn on 'Limit Ad Tracking.'
