Do you love delicious, out of this world burgers? In the near future, you're not alone. After we discover life beyond earth, a tense intergalactic battle for resources ensues.

A beam of light spreads through galaxy when Earth suddenly offers excellent burgers with premium toppings and ingredients. No other planet can compete with Earth's fresh, never frozen burgers delivered in just 10 seconds. Immediately all aliens lay down their arms, and pick up their phones, to order delicious burgers.

Earth is recruiting the best of the best to operate high speed burger delivery drones and save our galaxy.



Features:
-High speed twitch skill arcade game
-Unique 'sketched' art style
-Beat the 10 second guarantee for a better score
-Unlock new delivery drones by reaching secret high score mile stones. Drones vary in size, speed, handling, shape, and acceleration; to provide new challenges


Tips:
-Use both thrusters (touch both the left and right side of the screen at the same time) to move faster, and get a better delivery time
-Delivery time is very important for your final score, fast deliveries can lead to a big point bonus. Delivering cold food can lead to zero points, or even negative, if you're too slow
-Use controlled taps for fine control during difficult turns
